About Me

Chell Young is a Scottish artist currently in her 4th and final year studying Painting at Edinburgh College of Art, her work investigates the relationships we have with architectural space. Through model making she creates realistic small-scale interiors which disorientate and surprise, orchestrating perception through sculpture, film and photography.
